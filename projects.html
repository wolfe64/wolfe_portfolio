Illegal Wildlife Trade Dashboard

Description

This dashboard illustrates illegal wildlife trafficking using four Tableau visualizations. Filters allow exploration by taxa groups like amphibians and mammals. Key patterns, such as country-specific preferences and biome hotspots, are visible.

Analysis and Reflection

This dashboard is effective and interactive. Filters and animation help users engage with the issue. A key takeaway is how countries vary in taxa demand. The stacked bar chart shows tropical biomes experience more trafficking.

National Parks Recreational Visits: 2023

Description

This dashboard explores and ranks National Parks based on their 2023 recreational visit numbers. It includes a proportional dot map, stacked bar chart, a region-based tree map, and a line graph showing visitation trends.

Analysis and Reflection

This was an enjoyable project. One notable insight: Great Smoky Mountains National Park accounted for 14.39% of all visits, and 83% of Southeast region visits — likely due to its scenic location and proximity to tourism hubs.
