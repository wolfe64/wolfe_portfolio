About Me

I am a dedicated library professional with a passion for leveraging technology and data to drive innovation and solve complex problems. My expertise spans across various facets of library science, with a particular focus on relational databases, data visualization, and integrating cutting-edge technological advancements to enhance information accessibility and data managemement.

I have a special interest in working with spatial data, harnessing its power to uncover insights and develop creative solutions in diverse projects.

In my personal time, I enjoy spending time outdoors hiking and white water rafting, playing strategy card and table top games, and spending time with my pets.
